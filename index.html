About Us

RS Home Care is dedicated to delivering compassionate, professional, and personalized care that addresses the unique needs of every client. We understand that each individual requires a different level of attention, support, and care, which is why our services are carefully tailored to ensure comfort, dignity, and well-being. Our main branch in Theni and our second branch in Madurai allow us to provide accessible, community-focused care to families across the region.

Under the guidance of our experienced management team, led by Subasini, we combine years of expertise with a deeply personal approach, ensuring that every client feels valued, respected, and supported. From routine daily assistance to specialized healthcare support, RS Home Care prioritizes safety, reliability, and the highest standards of professional care. Our team is trained to handle a wide range of needs, including elderly care, post-hospital recovery, and chronic condition support, making us a trusted partner for families seeking peace of mind and exceptional home care services.

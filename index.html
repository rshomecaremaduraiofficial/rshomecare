About Us

We are committed to helping families find dependable, compassionate, and personalized support at home.

RS Home Nursing Care is dedicated to delivering compassionate, professional, and personalized care that addresses the unique needs of every client. We understand that each individual requires a different level of attention, support, and care, which is why our services are carefully tailored to ensure comfort, dignity, and well-being.

Our main branch in Theni and our second branch in Madurai allow us to provide accessible, community-focused care to families across the region. Under the guidance of our experienced management team, led by Subasini, we combine years of expertise with a deeply personal approach so every client feels valued, respected, and supported.

From routine daily assistance to specialized healthcare support, RS Home Care prioritizes safety, reliability, and the highest standards of professional care. Our team is trained to support elderly care, post-hospital recovery, chronic condition support, childcare assistance, cooking support, and household help—making us a trusted partner for families seeking peace of mind.

What Good Home Care Should Include

A few important things families often look for when choosing the right care support.

Clear communication Families should always know what support is being provided.
Safety and dignity Every task should be handled with care and respect.
Consistent routine A calm and reliable daily structure matters.
Qualified support Caregivers should be trained and dependable.
Personal attention Care should fit the person, not the other way around.
Family confidence Good care supports the whole household, not only the client.
